# Saving St. Martin's

St. Martin's Island, the only coral island in Bangladesh, was facing a silent crisis. Over the years, tourism and local waste had accumulated, threatening the fragile ecosystem and the marine life that calls the reef home.

## The Action

In early May 2026, Greenity Foundation organized its largest cleanup mission yet. Over **150 volunteers** joined forces with local residents to tackle the growing plastic problem.

- **2,000kg** of plastic waste removed.
* **100+** local businesses committed to a single-use plastic ban.
- **50** trash bins installed across the main beach areas.

## The Impact

This wasn't just about cleaning the beach; it was about changing mindsets. By working directly with the community, we've established a long-term monitoring system to ensure the corals remain protected for generations.

> "Nature doesn't need people, people need nature. We are simply giving the island a chance to breathe again." — *Greenity Volunteer*
